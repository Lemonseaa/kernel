# Business Lines

Business lines are where LoopHarness proves value in concrete domains.

They are not the core framework. They are applications of the core evidence harness.

## Current Business Lines

- [quant/](quant/): quant research and backtest evidence drills.
- [content/](content/): media/content growth direction.
- [demo/](demo/): temporary demo adapters such as the existing OPC Agent project.

## Rule

Business-line documents should answer:

```text
What workflow is being observed?
What evidence can be collected?
What metrics matter?
What baseline/candidate comparison is meaningful?
What human decision is supported?
```
